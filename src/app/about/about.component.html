Moon Koradji Records

Independent psychedelic music label Moon Koradji Records was founded in Ukraine in 2007 by Oleksandr Nikiienko, aka DJ Omsun. The Moon Koradji brand itself, however, has existed since 2000, when it began as a psytrance event organization that shaped the Ukrainian underground scene throughout the following decade.

The label is a unity of creative DJs and musicians - explorers of psychedelic music development. It follows its main purpose: to discover, collect, release, and produce high-quality music stuff for those, who appreciate the mysteries of psychedelic sound.

We welcome creative musicians and skilled sound scientists with fresh ideas for creating and delivering innovative musical experiences.

All the material will be appropriately collected and professionally evaluated.
