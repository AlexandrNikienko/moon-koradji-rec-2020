Moon Koradji Records

Independent record label Moon Koradji Records was founded in Ukraine in 2007 by Alexandr Nikienko aka DJ Omsun.

It is a unity of creative DJs and musicians - explorers of psychedelic music stream development. The label follows its basic purpose: to discover, collect, release, and produce qualitative music stuff for those, who appreciate the mysteries of psychedelic sound.

We welcome creative musicians, skillful scientists of music making, and new fresh ideas for making dancefloor sound in the rhythm of everyone's soul.

All the material will be appropriately collected and professionally evaluated.
