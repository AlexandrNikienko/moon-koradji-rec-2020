Moon Koradji Records

Independent record label Moon Koradji Records was founded in Ukraine in 2007 by Alexandr Nikienko a.k.a. dj Omsun.

It is a unity of creative DJs and musicians - explorers of psychedelic music stream development. Label is opened for cooperation and contribution with fresh music ideas, musicians and people interested in music production. Label follows its basic purpose: to discover, collect, release and produce qualitative music stuff for those, who appreciate mysteries of psychedelic sound.

We welcome creative musicians, skillful scientists of music making and new fresh ideas for making dance floor sound in the rhythm of everyone's soul.

All the material will be appropriately collected and professionally evaluated.
